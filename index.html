Press S to shield the grenades.
Shield rate is how much of the time you're shielding while a grenade is pulled. Success rate is how many grenades you've been shielding successfully.
The goal is to keep the shield rate low, while keeping the success rate to 100%.

Try to learn the timing at first, then play this mini game while doing something else (watch a movie, analyze a VOD, talk to someone, ...). You'll learn the timing of the grenades in no time.
